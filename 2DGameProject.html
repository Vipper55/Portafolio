The Story

About the Game

Hell and Above follows the journey of Dante, an explorer who, through mysterious circumstances, finds himself trapped in the circles of Dante Alighieri's Inferno. Your mission is clear but daunting: climb through each circle of hell and escape this eternal damnation.

This 2D platformer challenges players with time-based survival mechanics. Race against the clock as you navigate through treacherous platforms, dodge enemy attacks, and overcome the perils of each infernal circle. Every second counts, and every jump must be precise.

Developed as a university project, Hell and Above showcases advanced 2D platforming mechanics, enemy AI behavior, and level design inspired by classic literature. The game features fluid character controls with jump and dodge mechanics essential for survival in this time-pressured hellish landscape.

Behind the Scenes

Development Process

1

Concept & Literary Research

Studied Dante Alighieri's Divine Comedy, specifically the Inferno. Developed game concept around escaping the nine circles of hell with time-based mechanics.

2

Prototype Development

Created basic platforming mechanics: jump, dodge, and character movement. Implemented timer system and tested core gameplay loop.

3

Enemy AI Design

Developed enemy behavior patterns and attack systems. Balanced difficulty to create challenging but fair encounters.

4

Level Design

Created multiple levels representing different circles of hell. Each with unique challenges and visual themes inspired by the source material.

5

Playable Prototype & Trailer

Finalized university project with a fully playable prototype. Created official trailer showcasing gameplay mechanics and atmosphere.
