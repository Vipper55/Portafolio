The Legend

About Qori

"Qori" is an isometric adventure game that immerses you in the mysterious lands of the Andes. You embody the Devil Huma, a brave warrior who seeks to recover the Bowl of Abundance, stolen during the celebration of the Festival of the Sun.

Set in the majestic Gothic churches, monasteries, and catacombs of the Andes, this game will challenge you to solve intricate riddles and battle legendary creatures while unraveling the lost history of a forgotten civilization. The name "Qori" means "gold" in Quechua, symbolizing the precious artifact you must retrieve.

Developed as a university project, Qori features a playable prototype showcasing isometric gameplay, puzzle-solving mechanics, and combat encounters. "Qori" will take you on an unforgettable journey through the magic and mystery of the Andes.

Cultural Heritage

Inspiration & Authenticity

Honoring Peruvian culture through thoughtful game design

Qori represents a unique blend of Andean mythology and Gothic architecture, creating an atmospheric world where ancient legends come to life. The Festival of the Sun serves as the backdrop for Devil Huma's quest to recover the sacred Bowl of Abundance.

The development process involved careful research into both Andean cultural elements and Gothic architectural styles. This fusion creates a distinctive visual identity while maintaining respect for the rich cultural heritage of the Andes region.

Through Devil Huma's journey across churches, monasteries, and hidden catacombs, players experience a world where history, mythology, and mystery intertwine, offering both entertainment and cultural insight into Andean traditions.
